FAQ_LOG

Yes, candidates may apply for up to two roles. However, you will only be interviewed for one role per day. Your primary preference will be prioritized during deliberation.
Sessions are not video-recorded. Panel members take written notes. All evaluation data is stored securely and only accessible to the advisory board.
Missing your slot without a 24-hour advance notice to the General Secretary results in automatic disqualification. Documented medical emergencies may be considered for rescheduling.
Individual slot assignments will be emailed to shortlisted candidates by April 15, 2026. You will also find your slot posted on the CPC official Facebook page.
Only candidates applying for Technical Lead and R&D Lead require demonstrable programming or research experience. Administrative and Media roles do not require CP background.
